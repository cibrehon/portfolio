Bio

About

Ege Ilıcak (b. 1988, Ankara, Türkiye) is a street photographer based in Heidelberg, Germany. After completing his studies at Bilkent University, Department of Information Systems and Technologies, Ege developed his passion for photography and chose it as a second profession alongside his engineering work. Ege's work can be found in private collections across the world and has been featured in international photography magazines in the UK and Germany. In 2021, he took master classes from Yael Martinez at Magnum Photos in Paris. Ege is the winner of Leica Gallery Frankfurt Photography competitions of 2022 and 2023. He continues to capture the local frames of life.
